A 3D game engine I made for IT 485 - ST: Vulkan Game Programming. It uses Vulkan for 3D rendering and SDL for drawing/output and completely original physics and entity systems. It's a 3D platforming game, similar to the 3D Mario Bros. games. The assets are from League of Legends, a popular MOBA by Riot Games. The pictures you're seeing are one from the in-game editor, a favorite of the class, and from the playing of the first level. The engine currently supports entities, physics, loading and saving levels, animations, and editing of levels. Time for more lorem ipsum!
